Case Study

BASE ATX — From 5-Step Wall to 2-Click Booking

Boutique Fitness · East Austin, TX · Founder: Andrew Phillips

Ad Spend $3.2K/mo
Recovered $1,200/mo
Lift 38%
Timeline 5 days

The Problem

4 for $50 Ads Drove Traffic. The Booking Wall Killed It.

BASE ATX ran Instagram/Meta ads for their "4 for $50" intro offer and Founding Member pricing ($325 + $30/mo). Ads clicked. But the "Book First Class" button triggered a modal requiring name, email, and phone number before showing a single class time.

ClassPass users booked the same classes in two clicks. Direct traffic hit a five-step wall. The phone field alone caused ~40% drop-off.

No follow-up existed. Leads captured at 10 PM sat until morning. No SMS confirmation. No email sequence. No "spot held" automation. Just silence.

Friction Map

Four Friction Points. Tagged with Drop-Off %.

1

Phone-Field Wall (38%)

Required phone before showing a single class time. Cold traffic bounced. ClassPass users skipped it entirely.

2

Modal + Iframe (12%)

Modal → Mindbody iframe load failure on mobile. Users saw spinner, not schedule.

3

No Guest Path (8%)

No "Reserve spot, pay at studio" option. $50 intro required account creation first.

4

Zero Nurture (15%)

Email captured → generic newsletter. No sequence for: viewed schedule/didn't book, booked/didn't show, trial used/no membership.

The Fix

Built a Working n8n Flow in 5 Days

1

Public Schedule View

Inline calendar, no auth required. Users browse times before committing.

2

Reserve Spot Button

Holds spot 15 min, no phone required. Converts browsers to holders.

3

SMS + Email Flow

"Spot held 15 min" SMS + class prep email on submit. Deep link to rebook.

4

No-Show Branch

30 min SMS: "Life happens. Next class on us?" + deep link to rebook.

5

Trial-Used Branch

Day 3: "How'd it feel?" → Day 7 Founder offer ($99/mo lock-in).

6

UTM Preservation

Across Mindbody → ClassPass → CRM for true CAC.

Results

38% Lift. $1,200/Month Recovered.
