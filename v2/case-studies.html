Boutique Fitness — Austin, TX

BASE ATX

Ad Spend $3.2K/mo
Recovered $1,200/mo
Lift 38%
Timeline 5 days

The Problem

BASE ATX ran Instagram/Meta ads for their "4 for $50" intro offer and Founding Member pricing ($325 + $30/mo). Ads clicked. But the "Book First Class" button triggered a modal requiring name, email, and phone number before showing a single class time.

ClassPass users booked the same classes in two clicks. Direct traffic hit a five-step wall. The phone field alone caused ~40% drop-off.

No follow-up existed. Leads captured at 10 PM sat until morning. No SMS confirmation. No email sequence. No "spot held" automation.

The Audit

We mapped every click from ad to booked class. Four friction points tagged with drop-off %:

  • Phone field wall (38%) — Required before schedule view
  • Modal + iframe (12%) — Modal → Mindbody iframe load failure
  • No guest path (8%) — No "reserve spot, pay at studio" option
  • Zero nurture (15%) — No follow-up for no-shows, trial used, or schedule browsers

The Fix

Built a working n8n flow in 5 days:

  • Public schedule view — Inline calendar, no auth required
  • "Reserve Spot" button — Holds spot 15 min, no phone required
  • SMS + email flow — "Spot held 15 min" + class prep email on submit
  • No-show branch — 30 min SMS: "Life happens. Next class on us?" + deep link
  • Trial-used branch — Day 3 "How'd it feel?" → Day 7 Founder offer ($99/mo lock-in)
  • UTM preservation — Across Mindbody → ClassPass → CRM for true CAC

The Result

38% Lift in trial bookings
$1,200 Monthly recovered revenue
20%+ Cost-per-trial drop
"The phone field was killing us. ProvenLift found it in day one and fixed it in five." — Andrew Phillips, Founder, BASE ATX
